## FAQ

**Q: What if I miss a day?**
A: The challenge streak breaks, but you can keep going. The real enemy is quitting entirely.

**Q: Can I do multiple activities per day?**
A: Yes! Stack activities to level up faster.

**Q: What counts as "workout"?**
A: Anything that raises your heart rate for 20+ minutes. Walking counts.

**Q: Can I customize the XP values?**
A: Fork the prompt and make it yours. That's the point.
